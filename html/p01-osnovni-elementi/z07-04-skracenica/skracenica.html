Označavanjem skraćenica se mogu korisne informacije proslediti pregledačima, sistemima za prevođenje i sistemima za pretraživanje.

The W3C is an international community where Member organizations, a full-time staff, and the public work together to develop Web standards. Led by Web inventor Tim Berners-Lee and CEO Jeffrey Jaffe, W3C's mission is to lead the Web to its full potential.
